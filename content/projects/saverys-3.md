Manor Farm is a Grade II listed farmhouse on the outskirts of Chipping Campden. The owners sought a home that honoured the building's agricultural past while offering every modern comfort. We mixed antique painted furniture with hand-blocked fabrics, and let the building's original features — flagstone floors, low beams, deep windowsills — guide every decision.
