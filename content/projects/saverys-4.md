The Dower House is a handsome Georgian property where simplicity was the guiding principle. Every room was composed around the extraordinary light that floods through the tall sash windows. We chose natural linens, muted botanical prints, and a palette of soft creams and sage greens that shift gently through the seasons. The result is a home of uncommon calm.
